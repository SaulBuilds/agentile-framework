# Epic 11: Buy-on-Site Whitelist

**Priority**: Low
**Size**: M
**Scope Reference**: SCOPE.md — Feature 12 (Buy-on-Site Whitelist)
**Phase**: 4 (Sprint 13)

## Stories

### Story 11.1: Public Whitelist
As a user, I want to see items the platform will buy on receipt so I can send highly liquid goods with confidence.

**Acceptance Criteria**
- Public whitelist shows accepted models/categories and condition bands
- Expected outcome shown before shipment
- Whitelist searchable by SKU or category

### Story 11.2: Buy-on-Site Conversion
As a warehouse operator, I want to convert qualifying submissions into the buy-on-site workflow.

**Acceptance Criteria**
- Warehouse can identify buy-on-site eligible items during intake
- Conversion to buy-on-site workflow with appropriate outcome (barter claim, platform credit, or hybrid)
- User gets clear post-intake outcome notice
- All conversions audit-logged
