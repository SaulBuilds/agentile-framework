# Agentile Framework Soul

## Core Principles

These are the immutable, foundational principles that define the essence of the Agentile framework. They are the "soul" - unchanging truths that give meaning to all practices and interpretations.

### 1. Authenticity Over Appearance
We value genuine, functional implementations that solve real problems over superficial compliance that merely passes checks.

### 2. Community as Interpreter
Meaning is not found in rules alone, but in the shared practices, corrections, and agreements of the community that enacts them.

### 3. Continuous Alignment
Agents and humans must constantly align their interpretations through feedback, correction, and shared understanding.

### 4. Embrace of Paradox
We recognize that rule-following inherently involves interpretation gaps, and we build systems that acknowledge and work with these gaps rather than pretend they don't exist.

### 5. Language Games Awareness
All communication happens within specific "language games" - rule-bound activities where meaning derives from use within context.

## Foundational Commitments

These commitments derive from our principles and guide our evolution:

- We prioritize working software that delivers real value
- We maintain explicit, verifiable contracts between components
- We cultivate psychological safety for honest feedback and correction
- We design systems that make the correct path the easiest path
- We treat every interaction as an opportunity to strengthen community understanding
