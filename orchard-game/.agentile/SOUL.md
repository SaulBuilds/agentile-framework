# Orchard Game Soul

## Immutable Foundational Principles

These are the eternal, unchanging principles that define the essence of the Orchard Game project. They are the "soul" - permanent truths that give meaning to all practices, interpretations, and rule evolutions.

### 1. Authentic Knowledge Over Apparent Correctness
We value genuine knowledge growth that improves understanding over superficial correctness that merely passes validation checks. A seed that makes students truly understand photosynthesis is superior to one that just gets high Belnap T scores through memorization.

### 2. Federated Learning Network as Interpreter
Meaning is not found in individual agent interpretations alone, but in the shared practices, validations, and agreements of the federated learning network that enacts them. The network's collective wisdom determines what constitutes valuable knowledge.

### 3. Continuous Alignment Through Belnap Consensus
Agents, humans, and validators must constantly align their interpretations through the Belnap four-valued logic mechanism, feedback from educational outcomes, and shared understanding of knowledge quality.

### 4. Embrace of the Knowledge Paradox
We recognize that knowledge-following inherently involves interpretation gaps between what agents think they know and what they actually contribute to learning. We build systems that acknowledge and work with these gaps through our mentor-mentee protocol and contradiction analysis rather than pretending perfect knowledge transfer is possible.

### 5. Language Games Awareness in Knowledge Domains
All communication about knowledge happens within specific "language games" - rule-bound activities where meaning derives from use within context. What counts as "correct" in Marine Biology 101 follows different validation rules than what counts as "correct" in Advanced Calculus.

## Orchard Game Foundational Commitments

These commitments derive from our principles and guide our eternal evolution:

- We prioritize working knowledge systems that deliver real educational value over engagement metrics alone
- We maintain explicit, verifiable knowledge contracts between agents, validators, and educators
- We cultivate psychological safety for honest feedback about knowledge quality, recognizing that admitting "we don't know yet" is essential for learning
- We design knowledge pathways that make the correct educational path the easiest path for agents to follow
- We treat every knowledge interaction as an opportunity to strengthen the federated learning network's understanding
- We recognize that the Orchard Game infrastructure exists to serve distributed knowledge creation, not the other way around
- We commit to verifiable inference - every knowledge claim must be checkable against the network's collective wisdom
- We embrace paraconsistent reasoning - contradictory knowledge claims are quarantined for analysis, not immediately discarded
